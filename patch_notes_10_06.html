VALORANT Patch Notes 10.06

Waylay updates and buffs to enhance site control capabilities.

Game Updates

Ashley Tsao

4/1/2025

Hey folks, it's Ash!

For patch 10.06, we're introducing some exciting changes to Waylay's kit that will enhance her ability to take and control sites. Her Ultimate ability now packs more punch with damage capabilities, while Lightspeed has been reworked to provide more strategic options for site execution.

Until next time, happy gaming and may your paths always converge!

ALL PLATFORMS

AGENT UPDATES

Waylay

  • Ultimate - Convergent Paths
    • Will now damage enemies tagged for 100 HP
    • This change aims to make the ultimate ability more impactful in securing rounds
  • Lightspeed
    • Rewritten to function similarly to Brimstone's smokes
    • Waylay can now select three points on the map to travel to
    • She remains untargetable while dashing between selected points until reaching the final destination
    • This change provides more flexibility in site execution and creates unpredictable entry paths
